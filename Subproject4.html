National Forest Inventory Analysis


Project Focus and Main Objectives

Defining Sampling Methods for Forest Inventory, Analysing and Presenting Results

Details

Forest Inventory Analysis is always based on statistical sampling methods in order to provide a correct overview of the current status of forests.
Sampling methods and parameters are defined in the office and data collecting is being done in the field with special tools.
Data cleaning and analysis is performed annually.
Data is used for various purposes, such as as part of mandatory reports to FAO and other international projects.
It is also an important part of long-term forest planning on a national level.


My Contribution

I was a Lead Research Engineer in this project for two years. I was responsible for data cleaning, data analysis, developing new sampling methods and presenting results to international partners.
