Forestry Investment Analysis


Project Focus and Main Objectives

Analysis of Hardwood Plantations as Long-Term Investments. The project focused on the ROI can be expected by applying one of the most popular fast-growing species.

Details

Intensive hardwood plantations are useful on those lands where traditional short-rotation agricultural land-use cannot be performed profitably due to soil or climate conditions.
These tree plantations offer an alternative solution not just for farmers but for investors as well.
Hardwood plantations can be considered as highly profitable long-term investments (12-25 years) especially when professional knowledge meets economical perspective.
